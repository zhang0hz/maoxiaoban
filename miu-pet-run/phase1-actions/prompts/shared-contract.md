# Shared Miu Action Prompt Contract

Use the attached canonical base pet image as the identity source of truth.

Identity lock:

- Do not redesign Miu.
- Preserve the exact blue bicolor ragdoll cat identity: soft white fluffy body, cool gray-blue ears and upper-face mask, bright blue eyes, tiny pink nose, white blaze, fluffy cheeks.
- Keep every frame recognizably the same individual pet.
- Prefer subtle pose changes over identity drift.

Style contract:

- Codex digital pet sprite style.
- Compact chibi pixel-art-adjacent mascot.
- Chunky readable silhouette.
- Thick dark 1-2 px outline.
- Flat cel shading, limited palette, minimal tiny detail.
- No polished illustration, realistic animal portrait, anime key art, 3D render, glossy icon style, soft gradients, or detailed fur texture.

Sprite strip contract:

- One single horizontal strip.
- Separate full-body frames arranged left to right.
- One complete Miu pose per invisible slot.
- Pure flat magenta `#FF00FF` chroma-key background.
- No visible grid, text, labels, numbers, watermarks, borders, scenery, UI, speech bubbles, shadows, glows, motion trails, speed lines, dust, floor patches, or detached effects.
- No body part clipped.
- No pose may cross into a neighboring frame slot.
- Do not use magenta or magenta-adjacent colors in the pet.
