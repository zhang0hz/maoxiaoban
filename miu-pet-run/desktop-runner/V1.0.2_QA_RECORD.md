## Desktop Interaction Checklist

Use this list after launching `/Applications/猫小伴.app`:

- Foreground app overlap: put Finder or a browser window over the pet, click buttons, inputs, lists, and drag targets in the foreground app.
- Transparent area: click near the pet image but inside the pet window's transparent margin.
- Covered pet area: place another app over the visible pet area and click through the foreground app.
- Fullscreen / near-fullscreen: enter fullscreen or maximize a work window and confirm the hidden pet does not intercept clicks.
- Quiet work: use a large work window and confirm the pet stays non-disruptive while direct drag, click, right-click, and menu bar controls remain available when the pet is visible.
- Drag: drag the pet toward all screen edges and confirm it remains recoverable inside the visible screen.
- Reminder bubble: trigger or wait for a reminder near top and side edges and confirm all bubble buttons remain visible and clickable.
- Right-click menu: right-click the pet while it is visibly interactive and confirm the control menu opens.
- Multi-display: move the pet to each display and repeat overlap, fullscreen, drag, and reminder bubble checks.
